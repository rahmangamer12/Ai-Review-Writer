1. Stack aur Queue mein kya farq hai?

Stack (Shadi ki Plates): Shadi mein plates ek ke upar ek rakhi hoti hain. Jo plate sab se aakhir mein rakhi jati hai, wahi sab se pehle uthayi jati hai. Isko LIFO (Last In, First Out) kehte hain.

Queue (ATM ki Line): ATM ke bahar line lagi hoti hai. Jo banda sab se pehle aata hai, usay pehle paise milte hain. Isko FIFO (First In, First Out) kehte hain.
Paper mein likhna: Stack uses LIFO principle. Queue uses FIFO principle.

2. Compiler aur Interpreter ka farq?

Maan lo tumhein ek English kitaab parhni hai jo Urdu mein nahi hai.
Compiler: Ye wo dost hai jo aapse poori kitaab leta hai, aur agle din poori kitaab ka Urdu tarjuma ek sath la kar de deta hai. (Fast, translates whole code at once).
Interpreter: Ye wo dost hai jo aapke sath baithta hai, ek ek line parhta hai aur tarjuma karta hai. Agar koi mushkil lafz aaye toh wahi ruk jata hai. (Slow, translates line-by-line).
Paper mein likhna: Compiler translates whole program at once. Interpreter translates line-by-line.

3. For Loop vs While Loop

For Loop: Jab tumhein pehle se pata ho ke kitne chakkar lagane hain. (Jaise: Ground ke theek 5 chakkar lagao).
While Loop: Jab tumhein chakkar nahi pata, bas condition pata ho. (Jaise: Tab tak chakkar lagao jab tak thak na jao).
